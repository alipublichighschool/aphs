About Our School

Ali Public High School in Madni Colony, Mughalpura, Lahore, is dedicated to providing a caring and disciplined environment where students grow academically, morally and socially, from the primary years through matriculation.

Our approach blends a strong academic foundation with attention to character-building, so that every child leaves us prepared for the next stage of their education and for life.

"Our goal is simple: to give every child in our community access to quality education close to home, taught by teachers who genuinely care about their progress."

Mr. Akram Ali, Principal

Admission Process

  • Visit or Call the School Office

    Visit the school to collect an admission form and ask about seat availability for your child's class.

  • Submit Form & Documents

    Fill out the admission form and submit the child's birth certificate, previous school leaving certificate (if any) and recent photographs to the school office.

  • Assessment & Enrollment

    A short assessment or interview is held depending on the class applied for. Once cleared, complete the fee submission and your child is officially enrolled.

Have questions about admission? Contact the Office
